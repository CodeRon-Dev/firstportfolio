About Me

Web Developer!

As a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Information Technology and previous experience as a Site Acquisition Officer for over 3 years, I am excited to transition into the field of web development. My journey began with a strong academic background, where I honed my skills in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. While my previous role focused on site management and acquisition logistics, it instilled in me valuable skills in project coordination, problem-solving, and attention to detail—all of which I am eager to apply to my new career path.

Driven by a passion for technology and innovation, I am committed to expanding my proficiency in web development frameworks and tools. I am currently enhancing my knowledge through self-study and online courses, focusing on front-end development and responsive design principles. My goal is to leverage my IT background and newfound skills to create intuitive, user-friendly websites that enhance digital experiences. I am enthusiastic about contributing to dynamic development teams, bringing a fresh perspective and a dedication to continuous learning and growth in the ever-evolving field of web development.
